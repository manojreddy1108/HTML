Favourite Activities/Hobbies

Playing Sports

Sports is good for our physical health and maintaining mental stability. It also builds physical fitness, confidence and boost our ability towards any work. It also gives refreshment for the people who are in any trouble or stress or anxiety.

Playing sports can develop teamwork skills in every person. Meanwhile, Sports also teaches us how to work with others and learn how to utilize different players strengths and weakness. By winning or losing in sports, we will develop ways to manage highs and lows in our life.

Reading books

By reading books, we can grasp great knowledge and the ability of developing good ideas. It also gives us many lifetime experiences. We can also know many historical moments which happened in the past.

We can build our vocabulary by reading books. It improves brain connectivity and fights depression symptoms. Reading literally changes our mind and gives many different thoughts.

Spending time with family, friends or relatives

By spending time with family, friends or relatives, we will improve the bond and relationship between all of them. In case of pathetic situations, they will help us come out from those situations. By this, we can also share knowledge between them. We can work together, help each other in difficult situations.

We can also get emotional support from them all the time. Spending time with loved ones helps in reducing stress, boosts positivity, and improves our physical and mental health.
